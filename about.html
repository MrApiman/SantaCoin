What is SANTACOIN?

SANTACOIN is a community-first BEP-20 token designed to bring seasonal joy to the crypto space. It uses deflationary mechanics: a portion of each transaction is burned, another portion is reflected to holders. In November we run daily "Elf-drops" — micro-airdrops — and on Dec 24 we host a Secret-Santa giving away 100 × $100 to the Nice List.

Core Principles

  • Generosity: Events that reward participation and sharing.
  • Transparency: Auditable contracts and clear tokenomics.
  • Community: Built by holders, for holders.

Team

A small, focused crew of devs and marketing elves. Team tokens are vested and locked to align incentives.

Security Notes

Smart contract audits are planned. Never share your seed phrase; we will never DM asking for private keys.
