What is SANTACOIN?

A deflationary BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain. 8 % of every transaction is redistributed to holders; 3 % is burned forever. Throughout November, elves drop random gifts. On Christmas Eve Santa gifts 100 × 100 USD.
